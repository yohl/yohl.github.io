US Market Research - SharePoint Design

Brief:

I was rehired shortly after my summer internship at UCB to work for a different department. The US Marekt Research team needed someone to redesign their outdated SharePoint site to better fit their business needs.

These were their needs:

  • An organized method of storing project folders and the documents associated with them
  • A centralized portal for all external resources
  • An efficient way to share company files and information

Initial Design Concept:

The initial wireframing was done through Axure RP to show the beginning design of the home page, as well as other inner site pages.
