US Market Research - SharePoint Design

Brief:

I was rehired shortly after my summer internship at UCB under a different department. The US Market Research team needed to redesign their outdated SharePoint site to better fit their business need.

SharePoint is a Microsoft product that can create websites. It is utilized to share, organize, and store documents and informations.

These were their requirements:

  • An organized method of storing project folders and the documents associated with them.
  • A centralized portal for all external resources.
  • An efficient way to share company files and information.

Initial Design Concept:

The wireframing was designed through Axure RP to show the early concepts of the new US Market Research SharePoint site.

Implemented Design:

The implemented designs went through multiple iterations of change until it was comfortable for the team to utilize and navigate. The goal was to get where you needed to go in 2 clicks or less, and this design accomplished that by displaying all of the possible choices through links.

I was unable to display some of the functionalities, such as searching through the document library, due to the company's confidentiality policy.

However, the main functionality of the document library was the ability to filter all the project folders through the metadata tags set in the columns.
